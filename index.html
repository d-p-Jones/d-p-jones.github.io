About Me

Hi there, my name is David.
My journey in data analysis started with Excel, where I discovered its vast capabilities and pushed it beyond its limits using VBA to transform and display data in a way that made underlying patterns obvious. Over time, I have expanded my skillset to include powerful tools like SQL, R, and Python, which enable me to extract narratives hidden within datasets and present them in a compelling and accessible manner. I find it truly gratifying to uncover insights that might have otherwise gone unnoticed and share them with a wide audience. Thanks for taking the time to learn a little bit about me and my passion for working with data. I'm excited to continue exploring and discovering new ways to bring the stories within datasets to life.
